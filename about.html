About World of Tethys

World of Tethys started as a what-if: what if a science fantasy cared about ecosystems as much as empires? The result is a saga where roots, rivers, bonded-creatures, and cities all have agendas—and where one exile thrown off a sky-city ledge might be enough to break an old order.

I’m building this as a multi-book arc with companion shorts, audiobooks, and visual field cards. If you want to grow with the world from the beginning, you’re in the right place.
