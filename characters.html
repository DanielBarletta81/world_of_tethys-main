World of Tethys · Cast

The bonds holding Sky City together

The experiment in the clouds, the river clans below, the forests in between, and the creatures that stop obeying. Start with the four bonds at the heart of Season One, then meet the wider circle.

Featured bonds

Igzier, Stryker, Karys & Ravel

Sky City · Exile

Igzier

Mixed-lineage engineer who treats Sky City like a system on a slab. Thrown from the Weep for refusing to lie about his mentor’s “natural” death.

  • • Numbers-first, heart-late.
  • • Bonded with a reckless sky-hound.
  • • Still deciding if the City deserves saving.
Ashwing · Bonded creature

Stryker

Ptero–raptor “sky-hound” who dives after Igzier during the exile drop, then loses him in ashfall when Watcher stirs. Moose-sized, scarred, still thinks he’s a lap pet.

  • • Built for gliding, diving, and bad decisions.
  • • Tracks Igzier through caves, shelves, and ash.
  • • Center of the Ashwing sigil.
Greenhouse · POV

Karys

Greenhouse heir who maps roots and water flow while the Triumvirate edits reality. Tied to comfort by family, tied to change by conscience.

  • • Daughter of Lady Eldora.
  • • Sees where pipes—and policies—crack.
  • • One of the hands on the City’s scale.
Younger Woods · Healer

Ravel

Tea-drunk forest healer who hears color, tone, and “bruises in the air” instead of warnings. Quietly decides Igzier is worth keeping alive.

  • • Calls the Weep “screaming in cracked teal.”
  • • Talks to roots and a secretive archaeopteryx.
  • • Follows trail hum more than orders.
